Co-founders

Ikaika Smith and Ilikea Smith

Husband and wife co-founders Ikaika and Ilikea Smith started Dry Oar River Rafting after years of river rafting adventures together. They actually met at a river rafting instructor training camp back in 2010 in Colorado. They both have been certified instructors since 2010 and have taught and guided people on rivers all around the world!

Meet the Staff

Bill Jones

Bill comes to us from Hawaii. He has spent the past 5 years as a certified river rafting instructor with Dry Oar. He is especially great with the little ones, being a father of 3 children with his wife Janie who is also an instructor with us. His specialty is helping anyone overcome their fear, if they are afraid or if this is their first time riding the rapids.

Janie Jones

Janie is originally from Michigan. She is the wife of Bill. They met in Hawaii while she was on vacation. Janie has been river rafting since she was little. She's been working as a certified rafting instructor for 8 years, 5 of them being with Dry Oar. Like Bill, she is amazing with kids, but her specialty is teaching. She helps with re-certification of our entire staff!

Michael Moor

Michael is our resident daredevil. He has travelled all over the world riding the most dangerous rapids in all countries. Born in Nevada, he has fond memories of riding the rapids in the Grand Canyon with his family. He has 10 years of certification and specializes in safety. He is our newest staff member, just making a year so for with more to come!

Co-founders

Ikaika Smith and Ilikea Smith

Husband and wife co-founders Ikaika and Ilikea Smith started Dry Oar River Rafting
after years of river rafting adventures together. They actually met at a river rafting instructor training camp back in 2010
in Colorado. They both have been certified instructors since 2010 and have taught and guided people
on rivers all around the world!

Site Name

BabyandIWork

Site Purpose with Goals

Imagine: You just had a baby. Congratulations! However, you're a working mom. You work from home and don't have anyone
else to help you take care of the baby during your working hours. Maybe you're breastfeeding. Maybe you have a job in
which you're constantly taking phone calls and can't have a screaming and crying baby in the background. What do you do? BabyandIWork.com is a one-stop idea shop on items that can be useful towards allowing a mom to work from home WITH a newborn.
From work desks to baby carriers, bottle holders and baby swings. We'll let you know what can help you and provide you with
links to buy them if you want. We'll also give you ideas of how you can set up your workspace to work for you and for baby.
You can also join our community of mothers and share your ideas that have helped you! Being a working mom is hard enough as it is.
With all the things you have to figure out, don't let working at home with your baby be one of them.
It is possible and BabyandIWork.com will show you how!

Target Audience

Our target audience is specific: Post-partum mothers who work from home, but it will serve anyone who works from
home with no one to watch their newborn. These people are looking for solutions to a fairly new problem, thanks to COVID.
People are working from home now more than ever and that is the new norm. However, most people and families don't
have anyone to care for their child while they are working. This site will identify important baby items that can help
soothe and occupy baby along with links for these items. It will provide ideas for arranging your work environemnt so that
it is easier to work and care for baby. It will also have a community page so that mothers and more can share their ideas,
give product reviews, and share their experiences with eachother.
There is no other site like it, so there is possibility for very heavy traffic. Other sites assume that you have the option
to create your own schedule. That is not true for all work from home jobs.

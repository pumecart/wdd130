Site Name

Dry Oar River Rafting Adventures: Spring - Fall for One and All!

Site Purpose with Goals

Purpose is to set business apart from other river rafting companies in the area.
Most businesses in area are geared towards experienced rafters.
Site will show immediately through images that we are providing this experience for most ages.
Site will increase revenue as there is very little competition.

Target Audience

Families are our target audience= 4 years old and up.
People will come to the site to ensure that we do cater to families, to check the age range that we serve,
to look up prices and packages, and special discount days. Apart from this, there will be weather updates,
info on how to reschedule rides should the weather be bad on a particular day, and more.

Connect With Your People

There are so many things that others don't realize are going on in the background as a work-at-home mama with a newborn! Nothing like working to the tune of your newborn crying and possibly screaming because surprise, surprise! -- baby is hungry or wants to be held or needs a diaper change. Whatever it is, that baby needs your attention RIGHT NOW, and no customer or group meeting, or boss even, is going to change that. Not so easy typing out that report with one hand while the other arm is holding baby. Don't even get me started with sleep deprivation - because sleep is a distant memory -- sigh --. And yet there's sometimes an unspoken expectation to perform as good, if not better than before returning from your amazing maternity leave vacation because let's face it: you weren't working, so it was definitely a vacation! Does any of this resonate with you? Whatever you're going through, whatever you feel, join the BabyandIWork mamas community by entering your email below and prepare to vent, chat, CONNECT!

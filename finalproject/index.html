Congratulations & Welcome to the Club!

Congratulations mama on your newborn! Yes, let's take a deep breath and have a moment to
celebrate you and the fact that you just had a baby! Hopefully you take time to remind yourself
everyday just how special you are -- especially since that sacred mommy and baby time will soon
have to include WORK. And let's talk about work because that is specifically what this website
is all about, but not just any work. We're talking about work at home with your newborn!
If you're here it's because you're looking for solutions to questions like, "How am I supposed
to work AND care for my newborn at the same time?" You're looking for ideas on ways to refresh
your work space to suite you and your baby. You're also looking for other mamas who are going
through the same thing because lately you've been thinking, "I can't be the ONLY mama going
through these struggles!" You are not alone! We welcome you into this unique, but ever growing
group of mamas who are figuring it things out and sharing it all right here!
